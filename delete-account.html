Delete Account and Data

This page explains how users of Crypto Mining Empire can request deletion of their app account and associated data.

How to request account deletion

To request deletion of your account and associated game data, please send an email to:

Affirmion@gmail.com

Please include the following information:

  • The app name: Crypto Mining Empire
  • Your Google Play Games account name or the email address connected to your account, if available
  • A short message saying that you want your account and associated game data deleted

What data will be deleted

When your request is processed, we will delete or request deletion of account-related game data associated with your account, including:

  • Cloud Save progress
  • Game progress and balances stored online
  • Linked authentication/account identifiers used for saving your progress

Data that may be retained

Some data may be retained where required for legal, security, fraud prevention, billing, tax, or platform compliance reasons. Purchase records may also remain available through Google Play where required by Google or applicable law.

Processing time

We aim to process deletion requests within a reasonable time. We may contact you if we need additional information to verify or complete your request.
